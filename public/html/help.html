Help

General questions

Do I need an account to play?

Yes. You will need to register in order to access campaigns and characters.

How do I make a character?

On your personal home screen, go to the character section and press "Create Character" to get started.

How do I join a campaign?

You can join a public campaign by pressing the join button on the open campaign list. After you have joined a campaign, it will show up in your personal campaign list.
Characters

Which edition of DnD is used for character creation?

Currently, character creation is set to use 5th Edition Dungeons and Dragons.

Can I edit a character I've already made?

Yes! On your homepage, you can edit your character by pressing the blue pencil icon in your character list. If you are in the middle of an active campaign, you can edit your character on your character tab.

How many characters can I have?

As many as you would like! We currently have no limit for how many characters you can create.
Campaigns

How many campaigns can I have?

As many as you would like! We currently have no limit for how many campaigns you can have.

How do I start a campaign?

If you are the DM, join the campaign lobby page and then press "Start Campaign" to begin your active session.
Dungeon Master

How do I draw on the campaign canvas?

To draw on the campaign canvas, select the shape you want to draw from the "Shape" dropdown menu. Then, click and drag to draw the shape onto the canvas.

How do I move objects on the canvas?

To move objects on the canvas, select the desired object in the Object panel and move the object with the mouse.

How do I remove objects from the canvas?

To remove objects from the canvas, open the Objects Panel and click the red "X" button next to the object you want to remove.
